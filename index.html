Overview

Charlie Pattinson is above all a curious mind.

Primarily a graduate researcher at the University of Melbourne, Charlie study's biological physics by building and using Diamond Voltage Imaging Microscopes to understand the electrical behaviour of biological systems.

In his spare time Charlie loves exploring his physical limits around running, swimming and trying his best with cycling to attempt triathlons and iron mans.

Charlie is a keen outdoorsman, loving hiking and getting out in nature to learn about how ecological systems work and the clever adaptions that have evolved in order to work harmoniously.

It the intersection of physics and nature, Charlie also has a keen interest in biomimicry or bioinspiration, learning how biological systems harness thousands of years of evolution to create timeless processes for operating sustainably.

In all of these avenues Charlie is also a keen educator and science communicator, relishing any opportunity to share his curiosity with others.
